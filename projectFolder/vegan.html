The Benefits of Going Vegan

Why Go Vegan?

  • Health: Vegan diets can lower the risk of heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and certain cancers.
  • Environment: Animal agriculture contributes to 14.5% of global greenhouse gas emissions. A vegan diet reduces your carbon footprint significantly.
  • Ethics: Choosing veganism spares countless animals from inhumane treatment each year.

Did you know? Switching to a vegan diet can save up to 200 animals per year and cut your dietary carbon emissions by 50%!

Veganism is More Doable Than Ever

Adopting a vegan lifestyle has never been easier:

  • Supermarkets now stock a variety of plant-based alternatives, from dairy-free milks to meat substitutes like Beyond Meat and Impossible Foods.
  • Popular restaurants and fast-food chains offer vegan options, such as Starbucks, Chipotle, and Burger King.
  • Online resources provide recipes, meal plans, and guides to help beginners transition smoothly.
